FAQ

Got any questions? We got you covered!

Where can I play?

We now have 15 Gamestate locations across Europe: 8 in the Netherlands, 3 in Belgium, 2 in Germany, 1 in Bratislava, and 1 in Wroclaw. For directions to each location, please visit the location page in the main menu of our website

How can I play?

Gaming at Gamestate is very simple, all you need is a playcard. You can get the playcard at the cash register or via our online webshop. On this card, you put credits, with witch you can start the individual games. Each game costs x amount of credits. The amount for each game is clearly indicated on the display. This generally runs from 1 to 5 credits, with the big exception of our VR games, which are 5 to 12 credits. So it depends entirely on which games are played, how quikly the balance runs out. With most games, You can save these for 1 year for great prizes! Furtermore, it is also possible to take a time play arragement. Pleasse note! Unfortunately, you will not recieve tickets with a time play arrangement.

Entrance fee?

The entrance is free! Feel free to walk in and check out the Gamestate concept.

How can I win tickets?

Some games have the possibility to win tickets, the better you play the more tickets you will receive. You can exchange your tickets at the ticket station.

Collaboration?

For all press requests, interviews and press publications you can contact info@gamestate.com

Can i buy my card onsite or should i buy it online?

It is posible to buy your card on site, though it is easier to buy it online because you don't have to stand in a line, and you can directly start gaming. Currently, only guests from The Netherlands, Belgium, and Germany can buy also their playcards online.

How long are my credits and tickets valid?

All credits and tickets are valid 12 months after purchase, so there is no need to spend all the credits in one time.

Age?

Everyone is welcome! All visitors under 14 years of age must be accompanied by an adult. Except for Gamestate Amsterdam, where all visitors under 18 years of age must ben accompanied by an adult.

Payment options on site?

In our Gamestate locations payment is possible with debit card, credit card (Mastercard or Visa) and cash.
